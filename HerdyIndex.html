About STBA Pontianak

STBA Pontianak is a quality-oriented private university. We always try to produce graduates who have excellence and competitiveness and are ready to develop in a global society. Satisfying customers, namely students is our goal. Our commitment:

  • Accepting potential students
  • Encouraging active and creative students
  • Provide adequate teaching and learning facilities and administrative services that satisfy customers
  • Presenting a dynamic curriculum according to industrial and technological developments
  • Collaborating with external parties selecting dedicated and accomplished teaching staff

We provide the best possible facilities for our students
